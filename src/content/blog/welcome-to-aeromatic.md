**Aeromatic Drone Services** is officially on the web at [aeromaticdrone.com](https://aeromaticdrone.com).

The new site includes a full **gallery** of aerial stills, service overview, and ways to request a shoot. We're based in **Bend, Oregon**, with regular work across Oregon, California, and Washington — and select projects farther out when travel makes sense.

**On the blog:** field notes, sample reels, and embedded video from our YouTube channel as we publish. To add a video to any post, set `youtubeId` in the frontmatter (the id from `youtube.com/watch?v=…`).

**Next up:** listing flythroughs, event coverage, and mapping case studies. [Get in touch](/contact/) if you have a property, site, or event in mind.
